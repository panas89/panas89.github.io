Hi, I am a PhD student at the Medical Imaging Informatics Laboratory.

My research interests involve the development of predictive Probabilistic Graphical Models (PGM) from observational clinical data. I work with Partially Observable Markov Decision Processes (POMDPs) and Inverse Reinforcement Learning (IRL). Additional research interests are machine learning, Deep Learning and how to teach AI to do machine learning.

I currenlty work in the domain of lung cancer screening.
